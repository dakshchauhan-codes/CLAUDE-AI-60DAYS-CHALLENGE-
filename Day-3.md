# Day 3 – Role-Based Prompting

## Objective

To understand how assigning different roles changes AI responses for the same question.

### Prompt 1

"How can I get a software engineering job at a startup?"

### Prompt 2

"Act as a Startup Founder and explain how I can get a software engineering job at a startup."

### Prompt 3

"Act as a Senior Software Developer and explain how I can get a software engineering job at a startup."

#Observations

🔹 Without a Role

* Generic career advice
* Focused on resumes, applications, and interview preparation

🔹 Startup Founder Role

* Focused on what founders look for in candidates
* Emphasized ownership, problem-solving, adaptability, and business impact

🔹 Senior Software Developer Role

* Focused on technical skills and coding expertise
* Highlighted projects, GitHub portfolio, open-source contributions, and interview preparation

#Key Learning

The same question generated different responses based on the assigned role. While the Startup Founder emphasized hiring mindset and value creation, the Senior Developer focused on technical growth and engineering skills.
