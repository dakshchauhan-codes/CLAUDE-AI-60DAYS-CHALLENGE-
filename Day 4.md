# Day 4 - Chain of Thought (CoT) Prompting

## Objective
To understand how Chain-of-Thought Prompting improves AI responses by encouraging step-by-step reasoning and structured thinking.

## What is Chain-of-Thought Prompting?
Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting is a technique where the AI is guided to think through a problem step by step before providing the final answer. This helps generate more accurate, logical, and detailed responses.

## Activity Performed
1. Studied the concept of Chain-of-Thought Prompting.
2. Opened Claude AI and started a new conversation.
3. Used the provided CoT prompt template.
4. Answered the questions asked by Claude.
5. Generated a personalized career roadmap.
6. Analyzed the roadmap and identified key insights.

## Key Learnings
- Structured prompts generate better outputs.
- Breaking down a problem into smaller steps improves reasoning.
- AI can create personalized plans based on user inputs.
- CoT prompting helps in complex problem-solving.

## Observations
- Claude asked relevant questions before generating the roadmap.
- The roadmap was customized according to my career goals.
- The response was more detailed compared to a simple prompt.

## Biggest Insight
The biggest insight was that a clear goal becomes much easier to achieve when it is broken down into small, actionable steps. Chain-of-Thought prompting helps create that structured path.
