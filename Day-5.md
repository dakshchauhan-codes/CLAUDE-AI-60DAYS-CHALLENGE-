Day 5 – Context-Aware Prompting Challenge
Objective

The goal of this challenge was to understand how context and personalization affect AI-generated responses. I compared the outputs of two different prompts: one generic prompt and one enhanced with personal context and specific details.

What I Did
Generated an output using Prompt A (without context).
Generated an output using Prompt B (with personalized context).
Saved screenshots of both outputs.
Compared the results to analyze the impact of contextual prompting.
Documented key observations and learnings.


Key Observations
Prompt A produced a general and broad response.
Prompt B delivered a more personalized, relevant, and actionable output.
Adding context helped the AI better understand the requirements and generate higher-quality results.
Specific details improved clarity, structure, and usefulness of the response.


Key Learnings
Context plays a crucial role in prompt engineering.
Personalized prompts lead to more accurate and meaningful outputs.
Well-structured prompts reduce ambiguity and improve response quality.
Prompt engineering is about providing clear instructions, context, and intent—not just asking questions.
